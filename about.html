Our Story

Founded in 1995, ABC Law Firm has grown from a small litigation practice to one of the most respected full-service law firms in the region. With offices in Rajshahi and Dhaka, we serve clients ranging from multinational corporations to individual litigants.

Our Approach

We combine traditional legal values with innovative technology to deliver efficient, effective representation. Our attorneys regularly publish in leading journals and teach at top law schools, ensuring our practice stays at the cutting edge of legal developments.

Commitment to Justice

We dedicate significant pro bono resources to access-to-justice initiatives, including our monthly legal clinic and representation of asylum seekers. Our firm has received the Legal Aid Excellence Award for three consecutive years.
